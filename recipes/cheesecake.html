Chocolate Cheesecake

A tasty chocolate dessert for any occassion

Indulge your taste buds with this decadent chocolate cheesecake recipe. This sumptuous dessert combines velvety, creamy cheesecake with a rich and luscious chocolatey twist.

Ingredients

For the biscuit base
  • 150g digestive biscuits
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 45g butter

For the cheesecake
  • 150g dark chocolate
  • 120ml double cream
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder
  • 200g full-fat cream cheese
  • 115g caster sugar
